## üìö Academic AI Tools

### SciSpace (Typeset)

**URL:** [typeset.io](https://typeset.io/)

Explain research papers in simple terms. Highlight sections and ask questions.

**Best for:** Understanding complex academic papers.

### Elicit

**URL:** [elicit.org](https://elicit.org/)

AI research assistant for systematic literature review. Finds and summarises papers.

**Best for:** Literature reviews, finding relevant papers.

### Consensus

**URL:** [consensus.app](https://consensus.app/)

AI that extracts findings from scientific papers. Answers questions with cited evidence.

**Best for:** Evidence synthesis, getting quick answers from research.

### ResearchRabbit

**URL:** [researchrabbit.ai](https://www.researchrabbit.ai/)

"Spotify for papers" - discover related research based on papers you like.

**Best for:** Finding related research, building bibliographies.

### Scholarcy

**URL:** [scholarcy.com](https://www.scholarcy.com/)

AI summarises research papers. Creates flashcards and key points.

**Best for:** Quick paper summaries.

### Explainpaper

**URL:** [explainpaper.com](https://www.explainpaper.com/)

Upload papers and get explanations of complex sections by highlighting text.

**Best for:** Understanding technical sections.

## üí° Using AI Tools Effectively

### Tip 1: Verify AI Outputs

AI can make mistakes or "hallucinate" information. Always verify important facts.

### Tip 2: Use Multiple Tools

Different tools have different strengths. Use Elicit for finding papers, SciSpace for understanding
them.

### Tip 3: Provide Context

Give AI tools context about your purpose for better results.

### Tip 4: Check Citations

When AI provides citations, verify they exist and say what the AI claims.
