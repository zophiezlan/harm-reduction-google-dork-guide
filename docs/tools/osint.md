## üí° Ethical Use Guidelines

### Appropriate Uses

- Finding official documents that should be public
- Researching policy history
- Locating evaluation reports
- Supporting advocacy work
- Academic research

### Inappropriate Uses

- Investigating individuals
- Finding personal information
- Circumventing access controls
- Anything that could harm people who use drugs
